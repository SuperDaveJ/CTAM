What Can You Do?

So, now that you’re aware of some of the cognitive errors caused by perceiving what you want to perceive, what do you think you can do to avoid making those errors?

Compare your ideas to those of our experts:

  • Be aware of both internal and external causes of your own and others’ behaviors.

  • Remember that you’re predisposed to:

    • Overestimate the role of internal factors and underestimate the role of external factors for other people’s negative behaviors

    • Underestimate the role of internal factors and overestimate the role of external factors for positive behaviors

  • Avoid overestimating your own influence in a given situation.
