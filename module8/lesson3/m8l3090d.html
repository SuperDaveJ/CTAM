Example

Overestimating our influence may have played a role in the 1998 U.S. failure to anticipate Indian nuclear weapons testing.

Compare what analysts thought to what the reality really was.

Analysis:

  • The election promises were just campaign rhetoric.

  • Economic sanctions and diplomatic pressure would dissuade India from joining the nuclear club.

Reality:

  • The new Indian Government was elected partly on promises it would add nuclear weapons to India's military arsenal.

  • Analysts overestimated the ability of U.S. policy to influence Indian decisions
