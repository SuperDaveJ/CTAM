Cognitive Errors are Hard to Overcome

Here's another optical illusion. In the graphic, you see two identical smiling faces - or do you? What happens when you select the Play button?

Cognitive errors are similar to optical illusions in that the error remains compelling even when you are fully aware of its nature. You may continue to process information in ways that you already know to be inaccurate. So, cognitive errors are exceedingly difficult to overcome.
