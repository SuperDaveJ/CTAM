What Can You Do?

So, now that you’re aware of some of the cognitive errors caused by favoring the first solution, what do you think you can do to avoid making those errors?

Compare your definition to those of our experts:

  • Double-check your final conclusions!

    • How close are they to the original estimate?
    • Is there a good reason for each conclusion?

  • Don’t discount data just because it isn’t vivid.

  • Check your conclusions to make sure they reflect all the data, not just the most vivid.
