What Can You Do?

So, now that you’re aware of some of the cognitive errors caused by the expectation of pattern similarity, what do you think you can do to avoid making those errors?

Compare your definition to those of our experts:

  • Don’t automatically assume a similarity between a cause and its effect. Remember, sometimes big things happen due to human weakness, confusion, or unintended consequences.

  • Remember that while your mind would like to find similarity in the patterns of events, sometimes that similarity of patterns is only in your mind.
