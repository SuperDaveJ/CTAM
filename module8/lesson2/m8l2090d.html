What Can You Do?

So, now that you're aware of some of the cognitive errors associated with overconfidence, what do you think you can do to avoid making those errors?

Compare your definition to those of our experts:

  • Be wary of how confident you are in your decisions.

  • Remember that in the difficult task of intelligence analysis, it is easy to believe your opinion is more accurate than these data would suggest.

  • Reduce overconfidence by considering reasons why your judgment might be wrong.

    • Use the Analysis of Competing Hypothesis Analytic Method to force yourself to try and disprove something, instead of trying to prove it.

    • Frame questions in a way that encourages disconfirming answers and opposing reasons.
