Work

While your work may not be on the front lines, similar pressures exist. Your work involves searching for the proverbial needle in the haystack. You’re asked to be both accurate and efficient in that search. As you try to balance the two, it helps to be aware of how the brain works and appreciate how automatic and powerful these processes are.

This balancing act between accuracy and efficiency is not an easy thing and the process is often beset by many pitfalls.
