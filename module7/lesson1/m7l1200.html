Perception and Organizing Information

You make decisions as you make your way in the world with incomplete data. Waiting for every shred of evidence would mean that you wouldn’t get anything done! You need to make lots of little decisions all the time. Most of the time, when you are wrong it doesn’t really matter. Thankfully, the overwhelming majority of our decisions are not life and death.

Interestingly, when you make life and death decisions, you often don’t treat them as such.
