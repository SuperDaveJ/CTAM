Evidence Examples

  • When TWA Flight 800 crashed, many people, including a CIA analyst assigned to the case, were convinced that the plane was destroyed by a missile. It took an enormous amount of convincing for them to finally believe that it was an internal plane mechanical malfunction.

  • When Pearl Harbor was attacked, people only slowly came to the realization of what was happening. At first, most people believed the attack was simply a drill, and they fit the evidence of the attack into that belief.
