Influential Factors

Your perception is influenced by two factors:
  • Biological/Neurological factors

  • Social factors
Biological/Neurological factors are how we are “hardwired.” These include your senses, age, health, fatigue, hunger, and biological cycles. These factors apply to everyone.

Social factors are the different social influences in our lives. These include cultural differences like nonverbal behaviors, odors, speech, use of silence, personal space, sex roles, gender roles and occupational roles. These factors differ from person to person.
