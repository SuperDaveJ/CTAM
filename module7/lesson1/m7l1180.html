Getting a Feeling

Have you ever gotten a phone call and before you even picked up the receiver, you knew it was bad news? Were you right or wrong?

Did you know the vast majority of the time when you think the news is bad, you are wrong? You get a feeling when the phone rings it will be bad news, and thankfully, most of the time that doesn’t happen. However, you discount your poor track record and persist in remembering the one time (if there ever was one) you were right.
