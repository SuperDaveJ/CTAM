Self-Concept and Perception

Your own self concept affects your perception because it guides the way you look at things. This includes your self-esteem, focus of control, and attribution.

  • Self esteem is described as a favorable or unfavorable attitude toward yourself. If you don't value yourself, then you could devalue what you think or perceive.
  • Focus of control means how you control things inside and outside of yourself. If you can't control yourself, then how can you effectively focus the analysis you are tasked to complete?
  • Attribution refers to how you construct explanations of other people's behavior. If you don't have a strong sense of attribution of yourself, then how can you analyze someone else's behavior?
