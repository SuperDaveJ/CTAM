Balancing Accuracy and Efficiency

Think about TSA security officers (screeners) and the very difficult job they have. Looking for weapons in your first bag of the day is easy. What about when you have seen 764 black bags? They all start to look the same. Add to that impatient, uncooperative, and sometimes hostile passengers, and it’s amazing they catch as much as they do.
