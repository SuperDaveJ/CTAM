Relevancy

Why is this relevant to intelligence analysis?
  • Because analysts make assumptions and expectations about the motivations of people and the processes of foreign governments. Events consistent with these expectations are perceived and processed easily, while events that contradict prevailing expectations tend to be ignored or distorted in perception.

  • Rather than getting into the “autopilot” mode, like you do when you drive, you should be aware of the ways in which your perception is impacting your perspective.
