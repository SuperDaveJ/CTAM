Assumptions and Inferences

Take a minute to read the example listed on the image on the right.

Is the assumption in the example always correct? No, probably not always. You need to be aware that while inferences come from assumptions, if the assumption is wrong, then the inference will be as well.

When you analyze information, you identify the inferences and assumptions in your thinking. You determine inferences that can be made from a situation and then figure out the generalization that led to the inference.
