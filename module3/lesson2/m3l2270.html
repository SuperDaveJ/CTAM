Fair-Minded Critical Thinker

Have you ever met a person who demonstrates careful, critical thought and project fairness, insight, and a genuine desire to serve the public good? These fair-minded critical thinkers model the kind of critical thinking behavior you need to foster within yourself.

Fair-minded critical thinkers:

  • Have a good understanding of their own nature, including their irrational “buttons” and their personal characteristics that may sometimes interfere with their work. 

  • Are not looking for perfection, but insight!
