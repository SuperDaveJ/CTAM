Emotional and Personal Attacks

The last consequence is the use of emotional and personal attacks.

Historically, you have seen this used in political campaigns. An example of this is the Daisy Girl Ad. This ad was one of the most controversial political ads ever used in a Presidential campaign. Its goal was to attack 1964 Presidential candidate Barry Goldwater for his statement on the possibility of using tactical nuclear weapons in Vietnam.

It only aired in once in 1964, but was aired repeatedly by news shows and had an impact on Johnson's landslide victory against Goldwater. It is a powerful example of an attack aimed at people's emotions.
