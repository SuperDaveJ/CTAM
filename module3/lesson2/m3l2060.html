Characteristics of Uncritical Thinkers

So what are some characteristics of uncritical thinkers?

Uncritical thinkers have personal beliefs based on:
  • Prejudices
  • Stereotypes
  • Caricatures
  • Oversimplifications
  • Sweeping generalizations
  • Illusions
  • Delusions
  • Rationalizations
For further reading, read the Paul and Elder article entitled “Uncritical Persons” from The Thinkers Guide to Fallacies. This book can be purchased from the Foundation for Critical Thinking's website: http://www.criticalthinking.org.
