Using Skilled Manipulation

The kind of critical thinking you just did is important to apply in many other situations; particularly if you have a feeling someone is trying to manipulate you.

It is also important to consider ways in which you might be using these techniques in your own writing. Sometimes it may be tempting to rely on these methods to better persuade your audience. Resist this urge! The best analysts are more fair-minded than skilled manipulators.

That brings us to the final type of thinker: the Fair-Minded Critical Thinker.
