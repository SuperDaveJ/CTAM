Dangers of Skilled Manipulation

Skilled manipulation is dangerous because it can so easily sway the average unskilled thinker. Skilled manipulators know this and are not above using their critical thinking skills to persuade and control other people.

You need to avoid being swayed by manipulators. To counter their influence, it helps to understand some of the techniques they use.

In their book, The Thinker's Guide to Fallacies, Drs. Paul and Elder talk about 44 Foul Ways to Win an Argument. The text of their discussion is found beginning on page 19 of the book. Paul and Elder list 41 separate dirty tricks which skilled manipulators use when communicating. A few examples of these dirty tricks include:

1. Appeal to Authority
2. Appeal to Popular Passions
3. Appeal to Fear
