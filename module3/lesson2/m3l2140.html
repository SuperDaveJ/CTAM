Historical Results of Uncritical Thinking

Extreme ethnocentric and/or nationalistic thinking has historically resulted in movements like the Nazis.

History tells us Adolph Hitler and the Nazi party took power in Germany through the electoral process. They dismantled the democracy, and the German people were content since it seemed Germany was on its way to once again being one of the greatest countries in the world. Public focus was on the country becoming great again, and other problems such as militarization, lost of freedoms and ultimately the death camps were thought to be lies perpetuated by others outside of Germany.
