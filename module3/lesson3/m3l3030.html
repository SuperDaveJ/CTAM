Importance of Critical Thinking

David Moore, summed up the importance of critical thinking to intelligence analysts quite nicely in his book, Critical Thinking and Intelligence Analysis.

“Intelligence analysts are engaged in an intellectual pursuit. They are trying to solve puzzles, resolve uncertainties, discover the nature and meaning of things that others would keep secret. They must have an entire intellectual apparatus to help them identify the problem, assess the parts they know and the part they do not, come up with an explanation of what is going on and then express it in a way that others—including an audience not steeped in their own techniques—can understand.” -David Moore
