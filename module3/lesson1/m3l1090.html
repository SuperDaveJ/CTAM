Tools and Techniques

Heuer argues that tools and techniques that prompt higher levels of critical thinking can greatly improve analysis on complex incomplete, ambiguous, and or deliberately distorted information.

In order to improve your analysis, you must look at alternative interpretations of the data you are analyzing, challenge any assumptions you are making about the data, and structure that information in a manner so that others can clearly understand your conclusion.

For further reading, (view) the Heuer article Psychology of Intelligence Analysis.
