Dangers of Non-critical Thinking

Good analysis depends on clear thinking and avoids being swayed by subjective arguments. It looks at the parts, and critical thinking demands that we look at all the angles, not just what is apparent.

In addition to the obvious consequences, what happens if you don’t learn to think critically? You:

  • Risk being swayed by the beliefs and assertions of the people around us. 

  • Risk taking actions based on ideas that aren’t firmly our own.

What makes this so challenging is that no one walks around saying, “Boy, I really don’t think for myself.”
