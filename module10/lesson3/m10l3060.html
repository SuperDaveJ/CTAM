Exercise

Let’s try a simple exercise using the first three steps of the Devil’s Advocacy Analytic Method. Consider the following:

"Suicide bombings are never justifiable."

Most people feel this statement is true. However, put yourself in someone else’s shoes; someone who may think that these types of bombings ARE justified. What might their reasons be?

Take a few minutes to play Devil’s Advocate. Keeping the steps in mind, jot down your ideas on a piece of paper. When you are ready to move on, select the graphic.
