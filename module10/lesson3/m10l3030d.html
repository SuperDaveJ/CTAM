The Devil's Advocacy Analytic Method Benefits

Cognitive Biases and Dominant Mindset

The application of the Devil’s Advocacy Analytic Method can:

  • Serve as a check on a dominant mindset

  • Bring forth contradictory evidence that may have been dismissed or not given proper weight due to a cognitive bias

  • Expose hidden assumptions that crate cognitive biases

  • Expose serious flaws in logic that go unrecognized under a dominant mindset

Key Assumptions and Analytic Judgements

The Devil's Advocacy Analytic Method process can help reaffirm confidence in the prevailing judgments by:

  • Explicitly challenging key assumptions to see if they will not hold up under some circumstances

  • Identifying any faulty logic or information that would undermine the key analytic judgments

  • Presenting alternative hypotheses that would explain the current body of information available to analysts
