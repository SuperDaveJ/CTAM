Satisficing

Here’s how it works: you look at an issue and identify what sounds like a viable hypothesis. You then look at the available evidence to determine whether or not it supports the hypothesis.

If the evidence seems to support the favorite hypothesis, you pat yourself on the back ("See, I knew it all along!") and look no further.

If it does not, you either reject the evidence as misleading or develop another hypothesis and go through the same procedure again.
