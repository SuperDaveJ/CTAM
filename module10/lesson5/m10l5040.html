Dangers of Satisficing

What are the dangers of approaching analysis this way?

If you recognize satisficing as a problem, why do you continue to fall into the pattern? One reason has to do with the limits of the human mind, which is

  • Easy to be led astray by the fact that there is so much evidence to support a particular point of view, and

  • Fails to recognize that most of this evidence is also consistent with other explanations or conclusions, and that these other alternatives have not been refuted
