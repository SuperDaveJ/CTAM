Module Summary

In this module, you learned about strategies to stimulate creative thought, and how critical and creative thinking interact in intelligence analysis. You learned about vertical and lateral thinking and their techniques, and how they work together and also differ from one another.

You also learned about the structured process for the Devil’s Advocacy Analytic Method, which can be a valuable tool in understanding the soundness of an analytic line. You learned how Red Teaming works and how it can add another perspective to the information you are analyzing. In addition, you learned about the ACH Analytic Method and how it can help you find the strongest analytic line.
