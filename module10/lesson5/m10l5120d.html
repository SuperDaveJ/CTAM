Step 5: Refine The Matrix

Reconsider the hypotheses and combine or reword as necessary.

Delete from the matrix items of evidence or assumptions that now seem unimportant or have no diagnostic value. Save these items in a separate list as a record of information that was considered.

Be sure you distinguish between hypotheses that appear to be disproved from those that are simply unproven, as we discussed the Hypothesis Generation method. Do not eliminate unproven hypotheses! You might need them later to support your analysis!

In this case, we are happy with what we have, so let’s move on.
