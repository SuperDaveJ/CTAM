Step 8: Perform A Sanity Check

The matrix shouldn’t dictate any conclusions to you, but should reflect your judgment about what is most likely. When you have completed it, perform a sanity check. Ask yourself:

  • Do you agree with the conclusions that matrix is suggesting? If not, why?

  • Is there evidence missing from the matrix that is important in your conclusion?

  • Do other perspectives outweigh what the matrix is saying?

You are not obligated to follow the conclusions of the matrix. But when you are done, it will serve as a shorthand record of your thinking and as an audit trail showing how you arrived at your conclusions.
