Vuja De

Another way to look at creativity is to look at the quote from biochemist Albert von Szent-Gyorgyi (bio):

"Discovery consists of seeing what everybody has seen and thinking what nobody else has thought."

You know the concept of déjà vu, the feeling that we have been there before? Tom Kelly, author of The 10 Faces of Innovation and general manager at IDEO, talks about the same concept using a unique term. He refers to "vuja de", seeing something for the first time even though you’ve seen it many times before. There are many examples of this ability to see things for the first time in our society.
