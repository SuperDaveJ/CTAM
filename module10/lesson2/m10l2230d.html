Cross Pollination Examples

Here are some examples of cross pollination. Notice how some very different ideas came together to provide creative solutions.

Question: In what way might transportation engineers use a shark to develop new transportation equipment?
Answer: The front-end of this high-speed train was designed based on sharks’ heads.

Question: What inspired the multi-colored designs for computers and phones?
Answer: Skittles candy

Question: Termite mounds keep a constant temperature to ensure eggs survive. What could we model after a termite mound?
Answer: Green Building in Zimbabwe, which uses 10% of the energy of conventional buildings. It has no
air-conditioning, but stays at a fairly constant temperature because of the design.
