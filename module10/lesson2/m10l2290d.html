Encourage Both Lateral and Vertical Thinking

Compared to vertical thinking, lateral thinking may seem very unstructured.

However both vertical and lateral thinking are important contributors to critical thinking. In fact, critical thinking is best when you learn to combine the two to generate and develop good ideas.

Take a moment to review vertical and lateral thinking and consider how you can apply both in preparing intelligence analyses.

Lateral Thinking

Lateral thinking is about moving sideways to come up with a new idea. But once that idea is found, it is still frail and needs strengthening.

Vertical Thinking

Vertical thinking, structured thinking, is necessary to deepen and strengthen the idea. Without that, it is likely to remain frail and unaccepted.
