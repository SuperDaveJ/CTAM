Crystal Ball Example

Suppose a crystal ball told us that the assumption that al Qaeda is planning future attacks on the U.S. Homeland is wrong. What scenario might be true instead?

Compare your ideas to these:

  • The statements against the U.S. are merely propaganda to increase their power base in the Middle East. They hope to radicalize and then take power in several Middle Eastern countries.

  • They hope to inspire homegrown terrorist groups to take up the cause and plan attacks, but not from a centralized command. They hope their rhetoric will cause the Western countries to tear themselves apart without them needing to plan large-scale attacks.
