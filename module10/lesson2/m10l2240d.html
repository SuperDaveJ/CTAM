How to Cross Pollinate?

The best way to cross pollinate is simply to expose your brain to a lot of different things to provide fodder for thought. What are some ways that you think you could do that?

Compare your ideas to these:

  • Browse bookstores, magazine racks, libraries, etc. for topics you might not normally read about.

  • Read sections of the newspaper you would normally ignore.

  • Find a hobby

  • Audit a course

  • Visit a museum

  • Share ideas with others and get their perspectives.
