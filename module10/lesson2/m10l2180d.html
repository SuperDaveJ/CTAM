Deliberate Provocation Example

Now, let’s try applying deliberate provocation to the following intelligence question:
Should all persons who are suspected of being terrorists be publicly identified?
Develop a deliberate provocation for that intelligence question, something that is seemingly crazy, and use it to move to new perspectives.

Compare your ideas to these:

  • Deliberate Provocation: Anyone who is suspected of being a terrorist should be identified by a specific mark on their clothing so they are easily recognizable.

  • Ideas: We may not be able to mark their clothing, but perhaps we could mark their identification or include a notation on their personal records.
