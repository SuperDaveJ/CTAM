Thinking Backwards Example

Now let’s try to shift our focus by thinking backwards.

Imagine that several domestic terrorist groups have joined together to launch a biological attack against shoppers at the Mall of America during the December shopping season. Thinking backwards, what kinds of things would you expect to see in the “past”?

Compare your ideas to these:

  • Surveillance at the Mall of America

  • Increasing communication between terrorist groups

  • Small scale tests of biological agents
