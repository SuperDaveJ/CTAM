How to Challenge the Rules

To force yourself out of familiar rules, you can use two structured analytic methods:

  • Devil’s Advocacy

  • Red Teaming

Take a minute to find out more about these analytic methods. You will learn how to apply these methods later in the course.

Devil’s Advocacy
In Devil’s Advocacy, you identify and challenge assumptions and weaknesses of the current analytic line.

Red Teaming
In Red Teaming, you act out the role of an adversary and become that person. “Living” the role helps breaks your normal mental set and permits you to relate facts and ideas to each other in ways that differ from habitual patterns.
