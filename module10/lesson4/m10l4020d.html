The Red Team Analytic Method Benefits

When analyzing a problem, you are usually an “observer”-you don’t have much participation in the issue. The Red Team Analytic Method transforms you into an “actor” operating within the adversary’s culture and political mindset.

This role play is useful when trying to replicate the mind-set of authoritarian leaders, terrorist cells, or other non-Western groups that operate under very different codes of behavior or motivations. It can also be useful when applied to criminal organizations in law enforcement problems.

This method also considers new or different information that might not have been factored into your traditional analysis. This information can include:

  • Familial ties

  • International political

  • Economic

  • Military pressures

For example, a Red Team Analytic Method expert might ask: "What would my peers, family, or tribe expect me to do in this situation?"

A Red Team Analytic Method expert may also ask his colleagues: "How do we perceive the external threats and opportunities?"

Finally, the Red Team Analytic Method can factor into its analysis the way in which personal power and status might influence behavior.
