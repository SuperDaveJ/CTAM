Step 3: Develop A List Of “First Person” Questions

The thieves might:

  • Call the credit card companies, using a legal device called a SpoofCard to disguise their voice and phone number, and activate the credit cards.

  • Once the companies fell for the ruse, use the cards for cash advances or to buy luxury items in Japan, Saudi Arabia and Dubai.

  • Pay to boost the card’s line of credit so they could go back for more cash.

  • Once every penny was spent, recycle the cards as backup identification to open new credit accounts.
