Step 1: Gather A Team Of Experts With In-Depth Cultural Knowledge

Step 1: Gather team of experts.

Without a sophisticated understanding of the culture, operational environment, and personal histories of the foreign group, members will not be able to behave or think like the enemy. If the team members don’t have this in-depth knowledge, they are more likely to get it wrong.

Members can never truly escape their own experiences and mind-sets, but this technique can at least prevent them from falling into "mirror-imaging" unconsciously.
