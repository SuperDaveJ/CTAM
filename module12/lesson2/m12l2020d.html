Types of Thinkers

Animation shows three boxes with the following titles and corresponding text displayed when each box is selected:

  • Uncritical persons/Intellectually unskilled thinkers: The danger of this kind of thinker is an inability to stand up for one’s own beliefs. These people tend to be easily swayed by arguments around them.


  • Skilled manipulators/Weak-sensed critical thinkers: While this group is made up of critical thinkers, their thinking is focused on controlling others. They use techniques like the “44 Dirty Tricks” to influence uncritical thinkers.


  • Fair-minded critical persons/Strong-sensed critical thinkers: This is the category analysts should be in, with an ability to take in large amounts of information, process it critically, and communicate judgments fairly.
