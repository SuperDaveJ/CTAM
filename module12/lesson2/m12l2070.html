Vertical and Lateral Thinking

Critical thinking or “vertical thinking” is when you start with an idea and dig deeper into it or build on it to arrive at a final conclusion.

Lateral thinking, or creative thinking, is moving sidewise from an original perspective rather than vertically. It’s about:

  • Generating new ideas

  • Jumping outside the vertical framework, and

  • "Leap-frogging" through several ideas to arrive at a reasonable perspective
