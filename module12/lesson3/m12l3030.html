Module Summary

To sum it all up, critical thinking is a personal skill that you, as an analyst, use in your job everyday. It involves more than the ability to accurately review information-it’s about truly looking at all the angles and developing the most likely findings for your policy or decision maker. Developing your skills is an ongoing process, and you should strive to improve them throughout your career. It truly benefits not only you, but everyone else around you as well.

Good luck in your thinking!
