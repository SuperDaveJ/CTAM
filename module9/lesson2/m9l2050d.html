Hindsight Bias Influences

  • Analysts: Overestimate the accuracy of their past judgments.

  • Consumers: Underestimate the value and quality of analysts’ efforts as well as how much they learned from intelligence reports.

  • Managers: Judge that events were more readily foreseeable than was in fact the case.

Hindsight bias is natural and it is difficult to overcome. It isn’t as simple as saying, “don’t do it anymore.” Simply informing people about it and encouraging them to avoid it is not enough to mitigate it.
