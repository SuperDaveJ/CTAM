Step 1: Identify the Hypotheses

Four police officers were killed when a roadside bomb exploded in Grozny, Chechnya, Russian prosecutors said Monday. An investigation into the incident by the Russian prosecutor's office of the Chechen Republic is under way, ITAR-TASS reported. Criminal proceedings have also been initiated in connection with the incident. According to Chechen police, the explosion happened at 11:10 p.m. Sunday, ITAR-TASS reported. It struck officers of the district's Interior Ministry as well as a patrol service regiment that was on duty. The explosion was detonated by a radio-controlled device at a crossroads.
