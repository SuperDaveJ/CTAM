Using Indicators of Change Method

The Indicators of Change Analytic Method should be used when:

  • When there are sharply divided views, an objective list of indicators can clarify disagreements: When you have sharply divided views on an issue, indicators of change can also depersonalize arguments by creating more objectivity. It can also clarify disagreements as long as everyone agrees on the criteria used to measure the issue under dispute.

  • When it is important to make a hypothesis about anticipated changes more transparent: This method also provides an objective baseline for tracking events or situations. When you compile a list of indicators, you actually can enhance the credibility of your analytic judgment as it allows your customer to track developments and build a more concrete case for action.
