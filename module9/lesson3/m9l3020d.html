Application of Indicators of Change Method

Situation Developing from Data: This method could be used when you would expect to see a situation developing from the data you have.

Some past examples where this method was applied could include:

  • Economic reform

  • Military modernization

  • Political instability
Tracking an Event: You can use this method whenever you need to track an event over time to monitor and evaluate changes. It supports other analytic methods, such as tracking mounting evidence to support a particular hypothesis, low-probability event or scenario.
