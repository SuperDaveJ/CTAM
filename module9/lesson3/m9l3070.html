Step 2: Create a List of Potential Activities

The next step is to create a list of potential activities expected for each hypothesis.

During this step, consider:

  • What would you expect to observe if the hypothesis were turning out to be true?

  • In some cases, it might be useful to also make lists to indicate expected observations if the hypotheses were turning out to be false.
