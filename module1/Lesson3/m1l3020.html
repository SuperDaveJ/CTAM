Terminology Resources

Working and collaborating in both worlds can really be a challenge, can’t it? Fortunately, there are resources you can use to help you understand some of the terms you may hear.

One of the first resources is the DHS Lexicon (view).

The lexicon document is arranged alphabetically, and includes the definitions, annotations, examples, synonyms and source of terms. It is a good resource to use for deciphering DHS terminology.
