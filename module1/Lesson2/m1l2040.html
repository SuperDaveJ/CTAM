Course Structure

Let’s talk about how the course is structured.

CTAM is designed in a 12 module format, with lessons and topics contained within each module. The table on the right tells you what each module covers, and gives you an estimated completion time for each.

The course ends with the Analytic Practice Exercise. This exercise allows you to practice applying critical and creative thinking concepts and techniques as well as analytic methods to a real-life simulated intelligence problem.
