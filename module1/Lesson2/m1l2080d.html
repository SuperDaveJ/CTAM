Theory and Analysis Approaches

The animation shows a book with the title ‘Sausage Theory and Intelligence Analysis.’ When the book is opened, the following text is displayed:

Sausage Theory

  • Noncritical thinkers eat the sausage without hesitation.

  • Noncritical thinkers don’t consider the facts or analysis about what is in the sausage-they just eat it!

  • Noncritical thinkers don’t consider the makeup of the sausage, where it’s been, how it was designed or developed, and what’s in it.


Intelligence Analysis

  • Critical thinkers have to know what’s in it before eating it.

  • Critical thinkers decide to eat the sausage only after considering all facts and analyzing their thinking.

  • Critical thinkers WANT to know what is in the sausage case, even if they don’t like the answer.
