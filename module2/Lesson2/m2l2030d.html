How Do You Identify a Threat Stream?

Look for Commonalities:
Analysts will look for commonalities among:

  • Subjects

  • Locations

  • Possible Targets

  • Means

Identify Likely Threats:
Analysts will identify likely threats, such as:

  • Surveillance of U.S. targets in preparation for attack

  • Strength checks of operations security

  • Intelligence gathering by opponents

Relate to Previous Traffic
Analysts will then relate this new traffic to previously received similar traffic for possible relationships.
