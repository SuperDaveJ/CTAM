Streams of Information

"While an individual message may not suggest a threat, when taken together—analyzed as a “stream” of messages—a stream of information pointing to a threat may emerge. There can be several streams contained within a single source.

Much of the DHS mission and that of its State, Local, Tribal and Territorial law enforcement partners includes protecting the United States from “dangerous people, things, and threats to infrastructure…” The ability to identify threat streams and how seemingly unrelated data may form a coherent whole is a critical challenge for analysts. Critical thinking is an important skill used in this task.

As you work through the course, you will become clearer about threat streams and the role you play in developing them.
