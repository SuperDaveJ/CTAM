Threat Stream Identification Questions

Data set #1: Customs and Border Protection Officers contacted the driver of a vehicle parked near CPB regional headquarters in Chula Vista CA where he was seen taking photographs of the complex. The driver had no criminal history and told officers he was an architecture student looking at buildings in the area for a project.

Data set #2: A foreign national crossing at San Ysidro Port of Entry was in possession of aerial photographs of the CBP heliport at Brown Field. He told CBP agents he was a helicopter buff and wanted photos for his scrapbook.

Data set #3: A U.S. Coast Guard port security team based in San Diego Bay stopped a recreational vessel for operating without the required equipment off from waters south of San Diego. When questioned, the boat’s occupants said they were out for a pleasure cruise. In their possession were green uniform pants and shirts lacking any identifying patches or badges. Occupants told agents the uniforms were to be used in an upcoming costume party. None of the occupants had ID on them.
