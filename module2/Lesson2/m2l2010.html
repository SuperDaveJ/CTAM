Critical Thinking and Threat Streams

In this lesson you will learn what threat streams are and how to identify them.

One of the first steps DHS analysts take when looking at information is to identify possible threats contained within it.

These threats, along with their supporting evidence, are referred to as “streams.” The term “threat stream” is one you will encounter many times through CTAM and during your career.

Grouped together, the threat streams of raw and processed intelligence data form a picture of a potential threat to the Homeland.
