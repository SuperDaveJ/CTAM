Unintended Outcomes

Intelligence Analysis is not an easy field! Sometimes, analysts do everything “right” but that doesn’t guarantee success. An example is the Yom Kippur War.

The Israelis, and the United States, made a wrong decision and were caught off-guard by the Syrian and Egyptian armies in the 1973 Yom Kippur War. Given all the available evidence, their decision was a very reasonable one.
