Another Definition

Image of Rodin’s Thinker statue as a watermark, overlaid with the following definition from Fisher’s book:

“…Reasonable, reflective thinking that is focused on deciding what to believe or do” .
–Alex Fisher, Critical Thinking: An Introduction, Cambridge Press, 2001

Callout for the word reflective says: “You should have an awareness of your thinking process.”

Callout for the phrase “deciding what to believe or do” says: “Remember, decision making is part of critical thinking.”
