What Do Universal Intellectual Standards Do?

The Universal Intellectual Standards are defined by Drs. Richard Paul and Linda Elder in their book, The Miniature Guide to Critical Thinking Concepts and Tools. You can evaluate reasoning (your own or anyone else’s) by applying these standards to each Element of Thought. If your reasoning does not meet these standards, it may be suspect or even faulty.
