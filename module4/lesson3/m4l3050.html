How to Bring the Elements and Standards Together

As you review pieces of intelligence, you’ll think through each element of thought and write down how it applies to the information presented. Once you have done this, you’ll next evaluate each element using the Universal Intellectual Standards table and the questions it raises. Does your element meet the standard and answer the associated question(s)? If you find that if it doesn’t meet the Standards in some way, you may need to rewrite it.
