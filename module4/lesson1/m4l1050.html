Question at Issue

Every time you look at information in an attempt to analyze it, you need to identify at least one question to answer or one problem to be resolved. Questioning is an art. If you are not clear about what you are asking or how it applies to the purpose or goal, you will not be able to find a reasonable or usable answer!
