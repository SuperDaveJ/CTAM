What Is a Hypothesis?

You may already be familiar with the term “hypothesis.” What do you think it means? Pause for a moment and consider your definition.

Here is how Richards J. Heuer defines hypothesis in Psychology of Intelligence Analysis:
A potential explanation or conclusion that is to be tested by collecting and presenting evidence.

Here are some other definitions:

  • One possible explanation for the available evidence

  • A statement reflecting an analytic judgment, which can help guide and order the analytic process
