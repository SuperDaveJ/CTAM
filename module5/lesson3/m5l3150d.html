What Do You Think?

Based on what you’ve learned so far about generating hypotheses, why do you think the Brainstorming Analytical Method is so important to generating good hypotheses?

Think about your answer to this question.

Expert response:
From Richards J. Heuer:

  • “Intelligence judgments require the ability to imagine possible causes and outcomes of a current situation. All possible outcomes are not given. The analyst must think of them by imagining scenarios that explicate how they might come about.”

  • “Brainstorming in a group stimulates the imagination and may bring out possibilities that individual members of the group had not thought of.”
