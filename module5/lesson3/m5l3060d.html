Generating an Hypothesis
Practice 1

Develop an hypothesis about the statement:
Your superior says that as the number of years of driving experience people have increases, the number of speeding tickets they receive decreases.

Apply the four-step process:

  1. Identify the major problem.
  2. Make an educated guess.
  3. Identify the major variables.
  4. Write a simple, declarative statement.
Expert response:
More experienced drivers don’t speed.
