Characteristics of Hypotheses

The animation shows graphic of an analyst’s notebook or computer screen with a hypothesis written on it. Question mark callouts point to the hypothesis, which when selected, display the following text:

The characteristics of a typical hypothesis:

  • Reflect an analytic judgment, not a reporting of facts

  • Are phrased as statements, not questions

  • Provide a potential explanation distinct from other explanations

  • Need to be tested against new evidence

  • Represent a jump beyond the available information to provide explanations based on analysis
