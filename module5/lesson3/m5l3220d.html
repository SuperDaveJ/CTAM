The ACH Matrix

Here’s how to use the ACH matrix:

  • List your hypotheses across the top of the matrix and your evidence for each hypothesis down the side of the matrix

  • Evaluate each piece of evidence for that hypothesis

  • Rank its strength and relevance to the hypothesis. For purposes of this matrix, +1 means "very consistent", -1 means "very inconsistent", and 0 means "not applicable".

  • Identify the strongest and weakest hypothesis
