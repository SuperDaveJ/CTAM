How to Frame the Intelligence Question

The animation shows images of question marks that can each be selected to display the types of questions you can ask to ensure your question is clear and precise. The following questions are displayed after selecting each question mark image:

  • What is the question you are trying to answer?

  • What important questions are included in what you are looking at?

  • Is the intelligence question being asked in the best possible way?

  • Is the question asked clear or is it complex?

  • Does the question need further explanation?

  • How do you see the question?

  • What kind of question is being asked? Is it historical, scientific, ethical, political, or economic?
