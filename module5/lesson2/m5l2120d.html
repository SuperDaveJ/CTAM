Step 1. Set Ground Rules

In foreground, you can see a group of people discussing. In background flipchart shows the following bullet points:

Ground Rules:

  • Never censor anyone’s idea

  • Generate ideas without evaluating them

  • Give yourself enough time

  • Involve at least one “outsider” in the process

  • Write everything down, even ideas that seem
    far-fetched

  • Allow for a pause while thinking of new ideas

  • Do not discard outliers. They may be useless noise or the germ of a new idea that deserves further elaboration
