How Does Brainstorming Help Critical Thinking?

Have you ever tried brainstorming before? Try it now. Based on what you know about brainstorming, think about how it can help you structure your thinking. What value does it add to your thought process?

Think about your answer to this question and write it down.

Here are what others have said about the benefits of brainstorming:

Brainstorming:

  • Helps maximize your creativity

  • Helps you step outside of your usual mindset

  • Produces a wider range of ideas

  • Allows multiple perspectives on a single problem

  • Forces you to suspend typical judgment and think more divergently

  • Gives permission to think “outside the box”

  • Prevents premature consensus around a single idea or thought
