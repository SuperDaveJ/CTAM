Case Study

Let’s try this method out with a scenario where you need to frame the question. Follow the steps below:

  1. Review your roles, mission, and assignment listed on the clipboard.
  2. Play the video.
  3. Print out the Brainstorming Worksheet (view).
  4. Complete the worksheet to frame your question. If you can, assemble some colleagues to help you brainstorm. If you are alone, list as many ideas about the video on the worksheet as you can.
