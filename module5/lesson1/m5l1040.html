Determining the Direction of Your Thinking

When you begin to look at a problem, you must first define the right intelligence question. This question determines the direction of your thinking. Consider this example
from The Thinker’s Toolkit by Morgan Jones: A man thought his waterbed was leaking.
Which question would best help him solve this problem?

Select your choice, then select Submit. You will be given two attempts to answer the question.
If the question is answered incorrectly twice, then the correct answer will be automatically selected for you.
