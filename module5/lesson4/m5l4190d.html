Testing Implications

Just like you did when testing the implications of your data, you ask questions to test the implications of your conclusions. What questions might you ask to test your implications?

Compare your ideas to those of our experts:

  • If I recommend the following action, what things might happen?

  • If I do not recommend the action, what might happen?

  • How significant is the implication and who and what are affected?

  • What unintended consequences might occur from the recommended action?
