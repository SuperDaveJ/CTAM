Analytic Exercise
You should now apply the indicators of change analytic method. Don't remember what this is? These are pieces of evidence you should track which might indicate an emerging trend, for example. Use the Indicators of Change worksheet to help you. Make sure you:

  • Choose hypotheses that represent situations where mounting evidence over time could signal significant change.

  • Develop a list of indicators for each of your chosen hypotheses.

  • List potential expected activities for each indicator.
