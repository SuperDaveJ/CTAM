Another Example of Culture and Mindset

Let’s see another example of how culture impacts our mindsets. How would you group the words listed in this graphic?

Here’s one answer to the question! Dyirbal speakers, certain groups of Australian aborigines, would classify the words as:
  • males, kangaroo, the moon, rainbow, fish, spear
  • females, dogs, birds, fire, water, sun
  • figs, honey, wine, cigarettes
  • meat, bees, wind, mud
