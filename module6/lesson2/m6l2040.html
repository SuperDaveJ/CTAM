Influences

What you perceive, how readily you perceive it, and how you process this information after receiving it are also strongly influenced by:

  • Past experiences

  • Education

  • Culture

  • Values

  • Role requirements

  • Organizational norms

  • Specifics of the information received

Outsiders (non-group members) often perceive other groups as homogeneous. “All those [insert group] are the same.”
