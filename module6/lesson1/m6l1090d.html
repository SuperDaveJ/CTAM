Upside and Downside of Mindsets

Advantages:

  • Unavoidable, easy to form, and resistant to change

  • Largely unconscious

  • Distills subjects instantaneously

  • Ability to absorb new information without paralyzing us

  • Provides insight into complexity

  • Allow us to become experts



Disadvantages:

  • Unavoidable, easy to form, and resistant to change

  • Largely unconscious

  • Potential for tunnel-vision

  • Filter out potentially important information

  • Tendency toward bias

  • The more experience we have, the harder it is to unlearn
