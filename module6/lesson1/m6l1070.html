Are Mindsets Bad?

Mindsets aren’t bad; they are simply a fact of life, and they actually help you order your world. Without them, the complexity of world could be too overwhelming and too difficult to understand. You develop them to help you deal with the complexity, and they are a universal feature of cognition.

We also think of mindsets using the following terms:

  • Framework

  • Heuristics

  • Mental shortcut

  • Schema

  • Worldview
