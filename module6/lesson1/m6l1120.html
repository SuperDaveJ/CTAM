Lessons Learned from History

Intelligence analysts said the Soviet Union would never collapse, the Berlin Wall would never fall, Syria would never pull out of Lebanon, etc. Look what happened there!

How can we take the best from experienced thinkers and resist falling into the potential trap of thinking about our current conflicts and adversaries in outdated terms? Because of our mindsets, it is often said that we spend too much time preparing for wars we already fought rather than the ones to come. In other words, while the future often looks like the past, and mindsets help us understand that, we need to prepare for the possible, not just the probable.
