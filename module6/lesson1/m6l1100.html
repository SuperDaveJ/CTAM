Disadvantages of Mindsets

Although we have seen the advantages and disadvantages of having mindsets, experts caution that mindsets are not easy to overcome. Morgan Jones states that:

"Once a mindset has taken root, it is extremely difficult to dislodge because it is beyond the reach of our conscious mind. Unless we carefully analyze our thinking, we probably won’t be aware that we have a particular mind-set." -Morgan Jones, The Thinker’s Toolkit, 1998

Heuer further points out there is a disadvantage to having a mindset:

"The disadvantage of a mindset is that it can color and control our perception to the extent that an experienced specialist may be among the last to see what is really happening when events take a new and unexpected turn. When faced with a major paradigm shift, the analysts who know the most about a subject have the most to unlearn." -Richards J. Heuer, Psychology of Intelligence Analysis, 2006
