Looking at Mindsets

Let’s look at some mindsets which historically may have affected intelligence analysis in the past.

Remember the 60’s and hippies? How did the police and the business community react to someone with long hair and hippie-style dress when they encountered them? That mindset was immortalized in the 1970 song "Signs" by the Canadian rock group Five Man Electrical Band.

In schools today, many kids wear apparel that has been long associated with street gangs and gangster life. How would someone not familiar with juvenile culture react when seeing this apparel worn in schools?
